Design, code and write for a living

At 26, I left a nice agency job and started to build my first websites. I've been enjoying every single minute of my time since then. I'm now a 27 years old maker and I finally know what I'd like to be doing for the rest of my life. That feels awesome.

I currently live, play and work in Tokyo. I meet new people almost every day. People is my thing. People is the reason I love design. People is what gives a purpose to what I do, what we all do. I don't buy into trends that don't set people first. Not content, not users, people.

I wasn't smart enough to go to an Art School. I got an MBA instead. All I have is my obsession for details, a bunch of books, and my admiration for Japanese minimalism. Oh, before Tokyo, I was living in Dubai; that's where started my design career.

My perpetual will to discover the world is an incredibly rich & endless source of inspiration.

This website aims to present a topline collection of my work and thoughts. Powered by Jekyll, the type is set to FF Meta Serif Web Pro. You have a project for me? I'd be glad to hear from you.
